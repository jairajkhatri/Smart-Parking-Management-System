Arduino pins -> PERIPHERALS

D2 -> D7 of LCD
D3 -> D6 of LCD
D4 -> D5 of LCD
D5 -> D4 of LCD
D11 -> ENALBE OF LCD
D12 -> RS OF LCD
D7 -> GREEN LED
D6 -> RED LED
D8 -> ENTRY BUTTON
D13 -> EXIT BUTTON
D9 -> SERVO MOTOR PWM/CONTROL PIN 

NOTE: 220 ohm resistor is neccesary for LCD Anode pin to +5V for the LCD to work
